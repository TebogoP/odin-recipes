Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

A delicious and creamy peanut butter banana smoothie that's perfect for breakfast or a snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions/Steps

  1. Peel the banana and cut it into chunks.
  2. Add the banana chunks, peanut butter, milk, yogurt, and honey (if using) into a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth. If you prefer a colder smoothie, add a few ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
